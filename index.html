Biography

Mark Dennis was born in Middlesbrough, UK. He studied Music from an early age at school and then at Teesside University where he received a HND in Music Technology. His passion for trance music took him down the path of production from the age of 20 as a hobby. With over 20 tracks to his name, he has showcased some of his music on SoundCloud.
