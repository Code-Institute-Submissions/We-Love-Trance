Biography

Mark Dennis was born in Middlesbrough, UK. He studied Music from an early age at school and then at Teesside University where he received a HND in Music Technology.

Mark uses a variety of music production software to produce his sounds. These include Cubase, FL Studio and Ableton Live. Whilst DJing, he admits that the more traditional old school vinyl is his preferred option.

His passion for trance music took him down the path of production from the age of 20 as a hobby.

With over 20 tracks to his name, he has showcased some of his music on SoundCloud.These tracks can be played from the website directly on the Listen page. Don't forget to turn your speakers up because we love trance!
